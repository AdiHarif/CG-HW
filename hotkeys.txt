I don't always write long manuals, but when I do:


HOW TO USE:

Mouse:
LEFT_BUTTON (hold): Move camera around lookAt point
RIGHT_BUTTON: Open menu
SCROLL_UP: Zoom in
SCROLL_DOWN: Zoom out


Keyboard:
ESC: Exit program
TAB: Switch between selected models
W-A-S-D: Move selected models in world view
]: Activate next camera (cyclic)
[: Activate previous camera (cyclic)
P: Toggle camera projection (Ortho-Frustum)
DEL: Remove selected model
B-V-I-K-M-N: Rotate camera around lookAt point //DELETE
F-H-T-G: Strafe selected camera
U-J: Scale selected model(s)
Z-X: Change step size
ARROW_KEYS: Rotate selected model(s) (X, Y)
F1: Toggle drawing vertices
F2: Toggle drawing edges
F3: Toggle drawing bounding box(es)
F4: Toggle drawing vertex normals
F5: Toggle drawing face normals
F6: Toggle drawing cameras

Legend:
Green: Selected model(s)
Grey: Unselected model(s)
Red dot: (0, 0, 0)
Yellow +: Cameras
Purple: Vertex normals (of selected model(s))
Red: Face normals (of selected model(s))
White: Bounding box
